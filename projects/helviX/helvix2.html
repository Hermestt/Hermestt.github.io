SaaS B2B White label for steel centers

helviX wants to provide the steel service centers with a customized sales software With order management, online shop and document management.

From a single and complex file to a kanban like structure

By creating a kanban-like structure using Figma Teams, I started by helping helviX move away from a single design file to an easier and lightweight way to look and work with design mockups.

The results were good, the separation of topics made it easier to navigate and find what we were looking for. Loading files and navigating was free of lag and overload. The files with the handoff designs were free of clutter.

Quotes & Dynamic quotes

One of the main features of helviX platform is the Quote feature were the steel dealer makes an offer to the buyer request. To reduce the back-and-forth emails this feature helps the buyer to accept and decline the quote or even further accept only certain articles from the quote.
